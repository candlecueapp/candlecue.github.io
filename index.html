CandleCue keeps traders in rhythm with the market through precise time-based alerts — not just at candle close, but also before key transitions and within sub-timeframes. Ideal for day and swing traders seeking multi-timeframe clarity without staring at the clock.

Never Miss Your Candle Close Again.

CandleCue is built for traders who rely on structure and precise timing. With fully customizable alerts that adapt to your trading style, it helps you maintain discipline across sessions — whether you're trading 15-minute scalps or tracking 4-hour momentum — so you can stay focused, follow your plan, and avoid impulsive decisions — one candle at a time.

Features

  • Precise candle-close alerts for your main timeframe
  • Customizable pre-close alerts (e.g., 1 minute before) to prepare for key candle events
  • Layered sub-timeframe alerts within your main timeframe — like 5-minute closes inside a 15-minute candle, or hourly closes within a 4-hour candle — for enhanced multi-timeframe awareness
  • Audio and/or haptic signals to keep you in sync with the market — without constantly checking the screen
  • Runs reliably in the background — even when your device is locked or asleep
