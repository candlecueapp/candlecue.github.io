Welcome to CandleCue

CandleCue is a customizable, multi-layer notification app for serious traders. By layering strategically timed alerts across your trading timeframe, it helps you stay disciplined, follow your strategy, and avoid impulsive decisions—one candle at a time.

Features

  • Precise candle-close alerts for your selected timeframe
  • Customizable pre-close alerts (e.g., 1 minute before) to prepare for key candle events
  • Sub-timeframe alerts within your main timeframe—like 5-minute bar closes during a 15-minute candle—for enhanced multi-timeframe awareness
  • Audio and/or haptic signals to keep you in sync with the market—without constantly needing to check the screen
  • Runs reliably in the background—even when your device is locked or asleep
