Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. CandleCue is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account or login is required to use CandleCue.
  • We do not track your usage behavior or device information.
  • No third-party analytics or advertisements are included.
  • All alerts and settings are stored locally on your device.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
