CandleCue

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. CandleCue is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • CandleCue does not track usage, collect device information, or include any third-party analytics or advertisements.
  • All alerts and settings are stored locally on your device and are never shared or transmitted.

1. No Personal Data Collected

  • CandleCue does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personal identifiers.
  • There are no analytics tools, crash reporting services, or third-party SDKs integrated into the app.

2. Local Data Storage

All app settings, preferences, and alert schedules are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

3. Notifications

CandleCue uses local notifications scheduled by the app itself. These are processed entirely on your device and are not transmitted through any server.

4. Children's Privacy

CandleCue is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us .
